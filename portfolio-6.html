Quote Book

For this quote book I chose quotes from Dr. Steve Maraboli. He is a life-changing Speaker, bestselling Author. The reason why I chose his quotes is because his quotes are very powerful and positive. Those quotes usually give people more encourage and inspiration when they have problems or feel upset.

About Design

For the design I chose keywords to express the quote, and have the left page all made by hand, right pages are all digital. For the font I chose one digital font Caflisch Script Pro semibold because Steve Maraboli is a good writer so I chose Script Typeface looks like handwriting.
