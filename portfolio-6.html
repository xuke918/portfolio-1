Year of The Rooster

This year is the Rooster in Chinese culture. I used traditional Chinese painting to express the Rooster. This style allows me to discover more painting skills in Illustrator by using painting brushes.

About Design

As we all know, Canada Post published postal stamps every year. Based on this year is the Year of the Rooster. So I designed this postal stamp and also made a poster to show how it looks like.
