About Me

My name is Ke Xu. I am a graphic designer who like working in various fields of communication, with focus on illustration, motion and branding.
I was raised in China and now I am a third year student from the Algonquin College Graphic Design Program. This program has enabled me to build a solid foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical training in the field. I have obtained advanced knowledge of Adobe software including After Effect, Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign. I have passion for illustration, motion and branding. I love learning new things and trying my best to improve myself.
